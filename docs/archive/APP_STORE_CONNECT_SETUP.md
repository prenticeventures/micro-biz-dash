# App Store Connect Setup - Step by Step
## Creating Your App Listing

---

## üöÄ Step 1: Log Into App Store Connect

1. Go to: **https://appstoreconnect.apple.com**
2. Sign in with your **Apple Developer account** (the one you paid $99 for)
3. You should see the App Store Connect dashboard

---

## üì± Step 2: Create New App

1. Click **"My Apps"** in the top navigation
2. Click the **"+"** button (top left, next to "My Apps")
3. Select **"New App"**

---

## üìù Step 3: Fill Out App Information

You'll see a form. Fill it out like this:

### Required Fields:

**Platform:**
- Select **iOS** (should be selected by default)

**Name:**
- Enter: **Micro-Biz Dash**
- (This is what appears in the App Store - can be up to 30 characters)

**Primary Language:**
- Select **English (U.S.)** or your preferred English variant

**Bundle ID:**
- Click the dropdown
- Select: **com.microbizdash.app**
- (This should already be there since we configured it!)

**SKU:**
- Enter: **micro-biz-dash-001**
- (This is just a unique identifier - can be anything, won't be visible to users)

**User Access:**
- Select **Full Access** (unless you have a team and want to restrict)

---

## ‚úÖ Step 4: Create the App

1. Review all the information
2. Click **"Create"** button
3. Wait a moment - App Store Connect will create your app listing

---

## üéØ Step 5: Initial App Information

After creation, you'll be taken to your app's page. You'll see several tabs:

**1. App Information** (we'll fill this out)
**2. Pricing and Availability**
**3. Version Information**
**4. App Store** (screenshots, description, etc.)

Let's start with **App Information**:

### App Information Tab:

**Subtitle:** (Optional - 30 characters max)
- Example: "Survive the 2026 economy!"
- Or leave blank for now

**Category:**
- **Primary Category:** Select **Games** ‚Üí **Action**
- **Secondary Category:** (Optional) Select **Games** ‚Üí **Arcade**

**Content Rights:**
- Check the box: "I confirm that I have the rights to use all content in this app"

**Age Rating:**
- Click **"Edit"** next to Age Rating
- Complete the questionnaire (for a game like yours, likely will be **4+** or **9+**)
- Answer honestly - it's usually quick
- Click **"Save"**

---

## üí∞ Step 6: Pricing and Availability

Click the **"Pricing and Availability"** tab:

**Price:**
- Select **Free** (or set a price if you want to charge)

**Availability:**
- Select all countries (default) or specific ones
- Usually leave as "All countries"

**Click "Save"** at the top right

## üêõ Common Issues

**"Bundle ID not found"**
- Make sure you're using: `com.microbizdash.app`
- If it's not in the dropdown, you may need to create it first in the Developer Portal

**"Name already taken"**
- Try: "Micro-Biz Dash 2026" or add your name/company

**Can't find "New App" button**
- Make sure you're in "My Apps" section
- Check that your Apple Developer account is active
